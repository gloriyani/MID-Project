What is UNAI?

Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) is a Higher Education instution managed by the Seventh-day Adventist Church of the wastern Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelist of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

1)Faculty of Philosophy
2)Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3)Faculty of Econimics
4)Faculty of Nursing
5)Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6)Faculty of Information Technology.
